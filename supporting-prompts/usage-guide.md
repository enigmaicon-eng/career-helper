# Career Helper - Usage Guide

This guide helps users understand how to use the Career Helper skill effectively.

**New to this skill?** Check the **Quick Start section** in the main SKILL.md file for common requests and instant examples.

## Quick Start Examples

**"I want to review my LinkedIn profile for a [specific role]"**
→ I'll analyze your profile and provide specific optimization recommendations for target role positioning

**"Help me build a LinkedIn content strategy - I want to post consistently but don't know what to share"**
→ I'll help you identify authentic content pillars from your expertise, create a 4-week posting calendar, and build your engagement network

**"Help me optimize my CV for this job description"**
→ I'll run ATS analysis, identify keyword gaps, and rewrite your CV for maximum match

**"Who should I connect with on LinkedIn at [Company] for this role?"**
→ I'll identify 8-12 strategic people to connect with, prioritized in 3 tiers with personalized messaging

**"I have an interview next week at [Company], help me prepare"**
→ I'll research the company, generate role-specific questions with STAR answers, and create talking points

**"Research [Company Name] before I apply"**
→ I'll gather comprehensive intelligence: financials, culture, hiring manager, red flags, strategy

**"Create a full application plan for this Chief AI Officer role"**
→ I'll combine all capabilities: research, CV optimization, LinkedIn updates, interview prep, networking strategy

## What to Have Ready

Depending on what you need, gather:

**For LinkedIn Review:**
- Your LinkedIn profile URL
- Target role/level (e.g., "Senior Data Analyst" or "VP of Engineering")

**For LinkedIn Content Strategy Coaching:**
- Current role and years of experience
- What you're genuinely good at (expertise areas)
- Career goals (what you want to be known for)
- Target audience (who you want to reach)
- Time available per week for content

**For CV/ATS Optimization:**
- Your current CV (file path or paste text)
- Target job description (URL or paste text)

**For Interview Prep:**
- Your CV
- Job description
- Company name
- Interview stage (screening/technical/panel/final)

**For Company Research:**
- Company name
- Job description or role title (helpful but optional)

**For Networking Strategy:**
- Company name (or recruiter firm if anonymous role)
- Target role title
- Your LinkedIn profile URL
- Brief background summary

**For Full Application:**
- All of the above

## How It Works

1. **Tell me what you need** - I'll ask clarifying questions or refer you to Quick Start section in SKILL.md
2. **I'll load only what's needed** - Using @ symbol to load supporting prompts and templates on-demand
3. **Templates ensure consistency** - All outputs follow professional, structured templates with attribution
4. **Get actionable outputs** - Files saved to `career-outputs/` in your project directory
5. **Iterate together** - Refine based on feedback, dig deeper as needed

**You're in control** - Skip capabilities, go out of order, pivot mid-stream. This is a conversation, not a checklist.

### About the @ Symbol (Progressive Disclosure)

When you see `@` in the skill (e.g., `@supporting-prompts/company-research.md` or `@templates/cv-template.md`), this means the skill loads specialized context **only when needed**. This keeps the conversation efficient and focused.

- **Supporting prompts** provide specialized expertise (ATS optimization, company research, networking strategy)
- **Templates** ensure consistent, professional output structure with proper attribution
- You don't need to manually load these - the skill handles it based on your request

## What This Skill Can Do

### 1. LinkedIn Profile Optimization
- Analyze current profile against target roles
- Rewrite headline for search visibility
- Optimize about section with quantified impact
- Reorder top 3 skills for ATS/recruiter systems (RSC API)
- Audit experience entries for role positioning
- Review existing posts for audience alignment (reactive)
- Discoverability optimization for recruiters

### 1b. LinkedIn Content Strategy Coaching (NEW)
- Identify 3-5 authentic content pillars from your real expertise
- Develop sustainable 3x/week posting strategy
- Build engagement network (20-30 strategic connections by tier)
- Create 4-week content calendar with specific topics
- Thread series guidance (when/why to use multi-post sequences)
- Voice coaching (write authentically, not from templates)
- Professional, non-cheesy guidance grounded in your experience
- Tactical/Strategic/Story post mix for variety and engagement

### 2. CV/Resume ATS Optimization
- Extract keywords from job descriptions
- Analyze your CV match percentage
- Identify gaps and propose truthful additions
- Rewrite CV with ATS-safe formatting
- Optimize for three ranking modes: keyword, TF-IDF, semantic
- Ensure parsing compatibility (dates, headings, structure)
- LinkedIn/CV consistency checking

### 3. Company & Role Intelligence
- Company fundamentals: size, funding, leadership, financials
- Market position and competitive landscape
- Culture assessment (Glassdoor analysis)
- Recent developments and strategic initiatives
- Hiring context and team structure
- People intelligence: hiring manager identification
- Red flag assessment with risk scoring
- All research cited with sources and dates

### 4. Interview Preparation
- Generate 15-20 role-specific questions (not generic)
- STAR answer frameworks using your real experience
- 5-7 pre-prepared stories adaptable to multiple questions
- Intelligent questions to ask (by interviewer type)
- Key talking points to weave throughout
- Strategy for addressing gaps/concerns
- Post-interview follow-up templates
- Execution tips and timing guidance

### 5. Strategic Networking Intelligence
- Identify hiring managers and direct team members
- Find internal recruiters and key stakeholders
- Discover company alumni who share your background
- Map mutual connection paths for warm introductions
- Prioritize connections in 3 tiers with timing guidance
- Generate personalized connection messages
- Content engagement strategy before connecting
- Response handling and follow-up approaches

### 6. Application Strategy & Planning
- Timeline with milestones and follow-up schedule
- Stakeholder mapping and connection strategy
- Risk mitigation for identified gaps
- Cover letter frameworks
- Application question preparation
- Decision criteria and offer evaluation
- Alternative scenario planning
