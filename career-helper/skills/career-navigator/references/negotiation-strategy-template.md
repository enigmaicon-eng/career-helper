## Negotiation Scripts

### Phone Script

```
"Thank you so much for the offer - I'm genuinely excited about joining
{Company} and working with the team. I've had time to review the package
carefully, and I'd love to discuss a few elements.

Based on my research into the market and considering {your specific value},
I was hoping we could look at the base salary.

Specifically, I was thinking about {target amount}. Given my experience in
{specific relevant area} and the market rate for this role in {location},
I believe this better reflects the value I'll bring.

I want to make sure this works for both of us. What flexibility do you have?"
```

that works for everyone. Would you have time for a call this week?

Best regards,
{Your name}

## Handling Objections

### If they say: "This is our final offer"

**Response:**
```
"I understand, and I appreciate you being direct. Before we finalise, could
we explore {alternative component}? I want to make sure I'm set up for
success here."
```

### If they say: "We have salary bands"

**Response:**
```
"I understand you have structured bands. Could we explore:
- A sign-on bonus to bridge the gap?
- An early review at 6 months with defined success criteria?
- Flexibility in {equity/pension/holiday/other}?"
```

### If they say: "We need an answer by {tight deadline}"

**Response:**
```
"I'm very interested and want to give you a thoughtful answer. Could we
extend to {reasonable date}? This is a significant decision and I want to
enter fully committed."
```
