## Alternative Scenarios

### If Company Culture Discourages External Networking

**Adjust Strategy:**
- Focus on alumni connections only
- Connect with former employees (left 6+ months ago)
- Engage publicly with company content (likes/shares) without direct connections
- Let application speak for itself
- Build relationships post-offer if successful

### If You're Currently Employed and Need Discretion

**Adjust Strategy:**
- Use privacy settings carefully ("Only you" on activity)
- Don't announce job search
- Space connections over longer timeframe
- Consider connecting via email vs. LinkedIn
- Be selective about who you approach
- Wait until later in process for broader outreach

### If Recruiter-Led Search (Anonymous Company)

**Adjust Strategy:**
- Connect with recruitment firm consultants first
- Build relationship with recruiter before approaching company
- Ask for company reveal before broad networking
- Once company known, execute networking plan
- Use recruiter as champion/advocate
