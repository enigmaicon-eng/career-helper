## 6. CLARIFYING QUESTIONS (Must answer before final CV rewrite)

These are critical - without answers, any rewrite would violate the framework's "no hallucination" rule:

1. **What specific product(s) did you build/manage at JPMC?** (e.g., fraud detection AI, customer onboarding, trading platform feature)
2. **What are your key metrics?** Revenue impact, user counts, adoption rates, NPS improvements, cost savings - any numbers at all?
3. **What was the scale?** Team sizes, number of users, transaction volumes, data volumes migrated?
4. **What AI/ML solutions specifically?** Model type, use case, accuracy improvements, business impact?
5. **What is your educational background?** MBA? Engineering degree? Certifications (CSPO, SAFe, etc.)?
6. **What specific cloud products at Accenture?** AWS/Azure/GCP? What was the migration scope?
7. **What are your target companies and roles?** FAANG only? Open to unicorns? Specific level (Senior PM, Principal PM)?
8. **What is your current compensation?** This helps calibrate realistic target roles and negotiation strategy.
9. **Do you have a portfolio or case studies?** Published articles, side projects, patents?
10. **What tools do you use daily?** JIRA, Confluence, SQL, Tableau, Figma, Amplitude, etc.?
